Why custom

A custom piece is not just something to own. It becomes part of a memory.

Made around your story

Colours, symbols and details can be shaped around family, Country, identity, celebration or a meaningful moment.

Designed to be kept

Each piece is painted by hand and sealed with care, so it feels considered, finished and gift-worthy.

More personal than off-the-shelf

How it works

From idea to finished piece

A custom order is shaped with you, so the final piece feels personal, polished and ready for the moment you have in mind.

01

Tell me the occasion

Share the item, deadline, person, colours, story and any inspiration you already have.

02

Shape the design

LaToya works through the visual direction so the artwork suits the object and the meaning behind it.

03

Hand-painted detail

04
