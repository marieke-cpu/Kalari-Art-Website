Why it matters

Art can carry more than story. It can carry support, awareness and hope into the places that need it.

Fundraising

Original artworks can help create a meaningful centrepiece for auctions, gala nights and community events.

Awareness

Story-led artwork gives people a way to connect emotionally with a campaign, service or lived experience.

Connection

Each donated piece brings people together around story, Country, healing and care for community.

Fundraising with a piece of art

If you are running an auction, gala, awareness campaign or community fundraiser, get in touch to discuss whether a donated or commissioned artwork is the right fit.

01

Share the cause

Tell LaToya about the organisation, event, audience, timeline and how the artwork will support the fundraiser.

02

Shape the artwork

Discuss size, format and story so the piece feels connected to the purpose of the event.

03

Raise support

Use the artwork as a meaningful fundraising piece that helps people connect with the cause.

Have a cause in mind?
